October 25, 2022, new moon day
Gróttuviti Lighthouse in Iceland


Gróttuviti Lighthouse was established at Reykjavík in 1897. It has been guiding ships safely along Iceland’s coastline.









October 25, 2022, was a new moon day. The coast gets high tide very quickly, and the view of the sight to the path to the lighthouse immediately has changed. This recording is also a part of the “perfect circle.”
