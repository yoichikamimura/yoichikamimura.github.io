Fjallsárlón Glacier in Iceland


Fjallsárlón (Icelandic pronunciation is a glacier lake at the south end of the Icelandic glacier Vatnajökull. Fjallsárlón, which is part of the bigger glacier, reaches down to the water of the lake, and some icebergs are drifting by on its surface
